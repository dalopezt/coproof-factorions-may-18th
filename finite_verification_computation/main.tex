\textbf{Method.} The verification was carried out by an MPI-distributed computation across three cluster nodes (\texttt{node1}, \texttt{node2}, \texttt{node3}), partitioning the range $[0, 2{,}540{,}160]$ into chunks and evaluating the digit-factorial sum condition for every integer in each chunk.

\textbf{Evidence Summary.} The scan recovered exactly four factorions---$1$, $2$, $145$, and $40585$---yielding a total record count of $4$; all chunks were processed and the cluster verdict was deemed sufficient, confirming that no additional factorions exist in $[0, 2{,}540{,}160]$.
